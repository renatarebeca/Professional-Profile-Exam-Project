Ella App

Ella was created during a school project as part of the Digital Business Development Theme.

Ella is a community based app that provides users with a deeper understanding of depression and support them in helping their friend or family member who is fighting it. Moreover, we incorporated a conversational UI, Ella.

Note: This was only conceptualized as a business idea and graphically represented.

MMD Curriculum

Save the Great Northern Forest!


This website was created as part of a school project during my 2nd Semester on the AP Degree Programme in Multimedia Design and Communication at KEA.

During this project we developed a marketing campaign concept for Greenpeace Denmark.

We analysed every aspect of the communication situation, the sender, the market, the competitors and the receivers. Based upon our analysis, data, conclusions and formulated strategies, we conceptualised a marketing campaign and realise various elements for it: a prototype of a responsive campaign website and a promotional video.

Fairtrade App


This website was created as part of a school project during my 3rd Semester on the AP Degree Programme in Multimedia Design and Communication at KEA.

During this project, we developed a marketing campaign concept for Fairtrade Denmark. The campaign's purpose was to help ordinary people understand what is Fairtrade.

We realised various elements: a mobile app prototype, a 360° video presenting some of the app's features and other online and offline elements, social media entries, banners and posters. Our app offers a vast range of appealing content about Fairtrade while using new technologies (e.g. Augmented Reality).

Note: The prototype was created as a web app.
The prototype does not contain all conceptualized features.
